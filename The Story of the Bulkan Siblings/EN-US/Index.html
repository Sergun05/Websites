This is a story made by me and my friend and tulpa Ana.
It had started on 11/01/2019, when I wanted to do a story with her, we decided what the story would tell and how it would be.
It was finished on 24/01/2019, the writing of the chapters, not translation or revision.
And I and she started to revise it on 08/04/2020, but we are still changing and revising some things recently, in 2021.

It talks about two kids who are siblings, they are a girl and a boy.
Their names are Izaque Bulkan and Ana Bulkan.
They live out on adventures around the world, finding dungeons and treasures.
Maybe finding some exotic creatures too.
Good reading!

Tulpa:
Tulpa is a concept in mysticism and the paranormal of a being or object which is created through spiritual or mental powers. It was adapted by 20th-century theosophists from Tibetan sprul-pa (Tibetan: སྤྲུལ་པ་, Wylie: sprulpa) which means "emanation body." Modern practitioners use the term to refer to a type of willed imagined being which practitioners consider to be sentient and relatively independent.

Two adventurer siblings were walking in the forest as they always do, a little far from their house, they are a girl and a boy.
The boy is called Izaque Bulkan, the girl is called Ana Bulkan, Izaque haves brown hair, Ana has blond hair.
Izaque is the older brother, Ana is the younger one, they are living in the year 1571.
The two siblings are living on the planet Earth, in the country of Gemastria, state of Yolkland, close to the Folkloria city.
They see a weird hole on the ground, they discover that it is a hole that leads to a stairwell, and there is a big stone on top of the entrance.

The boy decides to remove the stone that was on top of the hole, after that, they go inside it.
Ana and Izaque continue to walk inside the dungeon, it is a little dark.
Izaque grabs a lit torch from the wall to illuminate the way.
The two walk a little more until they find a wall that looked like it was reconstructed, there are a lot of skulls and skeletons around it.
"Maybe there's something behind this wall, it looks soft," says Izaque.
"Yeah, let's walk more to see if we can find something to break it, bro," says Ana.
"Okay little sis, hehe," says Izaque, smiling, they continue to walk further into the dungeon.

Walking further, they find a pickax left by a corpse, the boy grabs it.
"They're giving us pieces to the puzzle that is breaking the wall?" asks Izaque, doubtful.
"I think so, let's go back to the wall to try to break it, bro," says Ana.
Then the siblings walk back to the wall, Izaque and Ana switch between hitting the wall with the pickax.
After some time, they manage to break the wall, pass through the hole and start to walk.
They discover that it is a hall that leads to a room, on the way, they find a lot of pictures and old portraits.
"Do you think there's someone still alive here?" asks the boy, doubtful.
"It must be a stalker [1], or a crazy collector..." replies the girl while looking at the pictures and portraits.

Suddenly, the boy notices a picture on his right and says, "Look! This is my grandpa".
The girl notices another picture and says, "There's a picture of my aunt here... There really is a stalker here".
"A stalker that knows my family and yours, why is he interested in them?" asks the boy in doubt.
"I don't know big bro, what your family has of importance?" asks the girl.
"Hmm, I don't know, they are a little rich, and... Maybe they have something important, a lot of story behind," explains the boy.
"Maybe this guy knows something that we don't..." says the girl.

They walk a little more and find a library with a lot of books, the boy grabs one, opens it, and starts to read.
"Tim Burton, Day 23 of February of 1567, went fishing, came home and went to slept..." the boy interrupts his reading to do a comment, "This looks like a journal or a book from someone who was spying another person," says the boy, gaping.
He starts to read a lot of books until he finds one of his grandfather.
"He has a boy by his side, seems to be his grandson, is looking at the waves of the sea..." the boy interrupts his reading again, "I remember exactly that day, this guy is really a stalker," says the boy, more surprised than ever.
"Okay, I think we should go discover who is this stalker... Do you think we will be in danger, big bro?" asks the girl, hugging her brother's arm, scared.
"No, I think we'll be fine, as long as we keep ourselves out of the sight of this stalker," replies Izaque, caressing the hair of Ana.

Suddenly, they hear a rumble and a stamp of feet.
"So brats are entering into my den eh?" asks a rough voice with a terrifying tone that appears from behind the duo, it is a man.
He puts his two hands on the necks of the duo and lifts them, the duo trembles from fear and the girl punches his face.
"I'll protect you, bro!" says Ana.
Ana takes a chair on the side and throws it at the man, it breaks, he screams in pain and gets distracted.
Izaque grabs a bread roll that was on the table, "Me too, little sis!" says him, then he hits the head of the man a lot of times.

They run to hide, the boy brings the bread roll and the girl brings an iron shell, they hide in a place of the dungeon.
"Where are you brats!" says the angry man while throwing everything in the air to find them.
"Oh, the things we get ourselves into, bro," says Ana trying to keep her voice low so as not to be discovered, she giggles a little.
"Shush, little sis," says Izaque.
The girl grabs a stone and throws it on the opposite side where they are to call the attention of the man.
"Hmm?" says the man while walking in the direction of the noise of the stone.
"Where are you, I know you're there, show up!" says the man enraged while he searches for the two on the place where the stone is.
Ana signals to the boy, telling him to go that way, so they can escape the man.

They walk slowly from out of the sight of the man, losing him.

They see that they are already far from the man and continue to walk.
"I think we lost him", says Ana, relieved, hugging Izaque to not get away from him.
"Yes, I'm glad you're safe, little sis," says Izaque.
"Me too, bro, hehe," says Ana smiling, who kisses Izaque's cheek.
They walk a little more, they find another hall and follow it.

They arrive at a dead end, there is a paper on the ground.
Izaque who is the wisest grabs the paper and reads, "In this room, there's something I like a lot, but I get rid of it if I need to, serves as fuel".
Then the boy looks for something that serves as fuel, he finds a dark wooden trunk lying on the ground, when he picks it up he hears a sound of a mechanism activating (Minecraft piston).
The boy notices that a hole has opened on the wall, he throws the log on the hole, it closes and a stairwell opens on the floor.
They go down the stairwell and enter a room where there is a big rock, but it looks soft and easy to break, Izaque finds a pickax.
Ana finds another one, she and Izaque start to hit the wall with the two pickaxes.
After some time hitting the wall, they manage to break it.
"Ladies first," says Izaque.
"Thanks, big bro, you're so gentle," says Ana, smiling.

Then the girl enters first and the boy comes in right after.
They walk a little, the girl steps on the ground and triggers a trap, with quick reflexes the boy can pull her back and an iron arrow with a chain shoots through the wall.
"T-Thanks for saving me big bro," says Ana, scared.
"Why there are so many traps here?" asks Ana, angry.
"Many? I think I only saw one, little sis," says Izaque.

With a quick reflex, the girl throws the boy to the other side of the room and dodges a lot of spikes that come from the ceiling.
"There's more, hehe," says Ana.
"You're okay big bro?" asks her.
"Yeah, thanks to you, little sis," replies Izaque, that gets up and cleans his clothes.
"I think the way is still long, so watch out where you step..." says Ana that giggles a little.
They keep walking until suddenly a spiked ball passes by them.
"Watch out!" screams Ana.
"You saved me again, thanks, little sis," says Izaque.
"Here you can't really be distracted, big bro," says Ana.

The siblings walk a little further and the traps stop appearing, they arrive at a tall, wide door.
Then Izaque pushes the door and they are blinded by a yellowish light that comes from the inside of the room.
"Where are we?" asks Ana.
"I don't know, maybe some special room, or a treasured place," replies Izaque.
After some time, they get used to the light and can see the room, they see a golden shiny sword stuck in a rock.
And also a stone sign is attached to the wall above the sword that says: "Only the worthy of this sword can wield it, only the person of the same blood as the creator is worthy enough to do so."
"Well, here I go," says Izaque, having faith in his strength and knowing that he is of the same blood as his grandfather.
Izaque walks forward and tries to lift the sword, after using a lot of strength, he manages to get the sword off the rock, he realizes that the scabbard is on the side.
He lifts the sword and it shines with the light of the candles around, looking like a real knight, he looks up, admiring the golden sword.
"Wow..." says Ana, surprised, looking mindfully at the blade of the sword, "You look like a real knight, big bro!" says her, smiling, admiring her brother.
"Thanks, little sis, hehe," says Izaque with his eyes shining, happy with his new weapon.

After verifying and looking around the room, both notices that this was the room of the grandfather of Izaque and this was his sword.
"Do you think he lived here? My grandpa, what he did here?" asks Izaque.
Then Ana replies, "I don't know, maybe studied a little, read books, and wrote too..."
"Is it just me or the stair is shining, big bro?" asks Ana.
"Looks like it is, I think it's for us to go up then," replies Izaque while giggling a little, he grabs the scabbard and wears it.
The glow of the stair gets intenser and the two start to walk on it, after some time walking, they arrive at an exit, there is some cool wind going on.
"Well, looks like that guy won't go after us anymore, little sis," says Izaque.
"He'll only go if he misses that... A shining sword..." says Ana, while looking at it.
"The sword has probably been there in that rock for many years, because he is not worthy to take it, so it's of no use to him," says Izaque.

The boy looks closely at the blade of the sword and sees the reflection of his grandfather in it, he looks back and sees no one.
He feels a connection to his grandfather and the sword as if it were destined to be his.
"Gee, you have a sword, and I don't even have a weapon, big bro..." says Ana a little sad.
"Hmm, I'm sure you'll find something for you, little sis, you deserve it," says Izaque, smiling and hugging his sister.
"Thanks, big bro, hehe," says Ana, returning the hug and beginning to smile again.
"I think we should go home and clean this dusty sword of yours, big bro," says her, who kisses Izaque's cheek.
"Okay, hehe," replies Izaque, smiling.
The two siblings start to go back home on foot, Izaque stores the sword on the scabbard that is on his waist.

"Where are we going this time, big bro?" asks Ana.
"I don't know, it's always someplace new, we always discover something new, little sis," replies Izaque, smiling.
They continue walking, always attentive to the sounds and noises around them, not to be caught off guard.
Then a sound of someone stepping on a dry leaf sounds behind them.
"Did you hear something, big bro?" asks Ana while looking behind her quickly.
"I did, little sis," says Izaque, agitated and looking everywhere.

Suddenly a deep voice surges behind them shouting "Rrrraaaa!" and trying to catch Ana.
But Ana quickly jumps forward and escapes from the hands of the man, she and Izaque start to run.
Until after much persecution, the two siblings manage to confuse the man and hide in a hole.
Izaque notices that the steps of the man are far away.
"Do you think he's gone, big bro?" asks Ana, worried.
"Maybe, be quieter please, little sis," replies Izaque, whispering, hearing the sounds.

Izaque grabs a stone and tries to throw it away from where the hole is.
He hears the sound of the stone falling to the ground in the distance and no more sound.
"I'm gonna take a look, stay down there, little sis," says Izaque, with that he puts his head outside of the hole to check the area.

They walk a little and do not find any signal of the man.
"Well, looks like he got tired of us little sis," says Izaque.
Ana replies relieved, "Oh, I'm glad we're safe, big bro".
"What should we do now little sis? Go back home?" asks Izaque.
Then Ana replies, "Let's be very careful and hope he doesn't see us, big bro".
"Good one, little sis," says Izaque.
They start walking slowly back home, it is almost dark.
After a while, they arrive at the door of the house and knock on it.
"Do you think we'll be scolded big bro?" asks Ana.
"Probably little sis, for staying out this late.
You don't need to say anything little sis, leave it to me," replies Izaque.

"Hey mom, d-don't you think we stayed out too late?" asks Izaque, scared to get scolded.

Izaque explains stuttering a little and with a shaky voice, "It's that we had a setback on the way, but nothing we couldn't face".
