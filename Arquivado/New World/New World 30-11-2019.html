New World

It's about a future, or a present, where technology is part of our day-by-day,

Name Guide

Old World(World):
It's how the beings in the Government call the Planet Earth(Milky Way Galaxy) when The Government didn't existed.

Old World(Beings):
It's how the beings in the Government call the beings that aren't from The Government and doesn't use the System(Holograms, Neural Network and Basic Pack).

New World(World):
It's how the beings in The Government call the Planet Earth(Milky Way Galaxy) when The Government started.

New World(Beings):
It's how the beings in The Government call the beings that are from The Government and use the System(Holograms, Neural Network and Basic Pack).




The Government

The Government is an entity that takes care of the rights of the beings all over the universe,
It helps non-human beings to get accepted in the society and in Planet Earth,
And creates laws, systems, and holograms for New World,
It also helps people live better because the New World Form or New World Being doesn't have the basic necessities that humans and similar beings have,
The Government is not the government of a city, state or country, its an entity managed by the creators of New World, and these managers are managed by an A.I.,
I(Izaque Sanvezzo) am one of the managers and technology creators of the New World, but I don't live in a New World World.




Laws

The laws of New World can't be broken, because it's a law that every New World user has to comply:
1. You cannot physically modify a being against their will.
2. You cannot take, grab or physically modify objects or things that don't belong to you.
3. You cannot modify physically structures that don't belong to you.




New World Form and Neural Network

The New World Form is like an ascension, you join the New World, and you'll be given the opportunity to evolve into this new form,
If you were a human-homo sapiens before the evolving, you will no longer have hunger, thirst, and sleepness,
You will have a stomach of infinite size, and it will be super fast, so you can eat whatever you want, and how much you want,
Also if you were born as a human, but also have a Dragon DNA, when you evolve to the New World Form,
You will be a Half-Human, Half-Dragon being, or a Human-Dragon Hybrid,
Your brain and mind will be super fast, like one billion computer processors working at the same time,
And your memory will be able to store all the data from the last 5000 years.
You will also receive a thing called Neural Network, connected to the System and the New World, it has a "Reader" too, you will, for example,
Be able to read 50000 words in some seconds or watch all movies produced in the last 50 years in some seconds,
Because the "Universal Story Library" and "Universal Movie Library" and the reader exists.
The New World Form was created so the beings could focus on other things such as writing, making music, drawing,
Listen to music, play games, watch things, talk on the internet, without being worried about their bodily necessities.




Holograms

Holograms are the main base of New World, they are touchable, they look like the Blue Cube in the header of the site, you can create them with your hand.
By opening your hand and imaging the hologram your want, or you can create them with your mind,

The holograms are just the visualization or the interface of the systems, and systems are something that modifies the world around us,
You can open a hologram to see your "UIH", you can open a hologram to modify the colors you see,
Like replace "yellow" by "blue", you can create holograms to store things like water, earth, sand, food,
The "Systematic Blocks"(Old name of the holograms) have some proprieties which you can modify, such as time, space, size,
You can, for example, store 10 ton of earth in a block that fits in your hand,
Or you could store all the water of Planet Earth in the same block, you can also freeze a food in time, so it will never rot.

Universal Identification Hologram(UIH)

This is the documentation of the Universal Identification Hologram(UIH)

IDs(Identification Technology):

Uid Format: [name] [surname] ([race][race_sex][age_in_planet_years][born_year_in_planet_years]),
Example: "Izaque Sanvezzo(hm172002)"

Universal Identification(Uid): "Uid is a way to give every sentient being a unique identification"
Format Expansion:
[name]: A word or a combination of words by which a being, place, or thing, a body or class, or any object of thought is designated, called, or known.

[surname]: A hereditary name common to all members of a family, as distinct from a given name.

[race]: An arbitrary classification of beings, sometimes, especially formerly,
based on any or a combination of various physical characteristics,
as skin color, facial form, or eye shape, and now frequently based on such genetic markers as blood groups.

[race_sex]: Either of the two main categories (male and female) into which beings are divided on the basis of their reproductive functions.

[age_in_planet_years]: A period of being life, measured by years from birth,
Usually marked by a certain stage or degree of mental or physical development and involving legal responsibility and capacity,
Some age numbers can be different from planet to planet,
Example: 79 years old in the planet Earth in the Milky Way galaxy is equal to 39 years old for the planet mars on the Milky Way galaxy.

[born_year_in_planet_years]: The year a being was born in the age number of the planet it was born.


Pid Format: [planet_name][planet_galaxy]([planet_population_number][planet_current_year]), Example: "EarthMW(7B2019)"

Planet Identification(Pid): "Pid is a way to give every planet with life a unique identification"
Format Expansion:
[planet_name]: A word or a combination of words by which a planet is designated, called, or known.

[planet_galaxy]: A physical space which a planet reside.

[planet_population_number]: The number of beings in a planet's area.

[planet_current_year]: The current year in the calendar numbers of that planet,
Example: When the "Homo sapiens" species landed on their Moon the current year in the planet Earth in the Milky Way galaxy was 1969.
