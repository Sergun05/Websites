Humans also lived inside a universe bubble, it was called the Human Universe Bubble (HUB).

The Mane Six, the main characters of the cartoon, Twilight Sparkle, Pinkie Pie, Fluttershy, Applejack, Rainbow Dash, and Rarity.

The sounds, sights, and smells were sent directly to a receptor called Signal Receptor and sent to the holographic brain and mind to be processed, heard, seen, or smelled.

The Signal Receptor existed since people got transformed into Hologram Souls.

The main interface was like a user interface where people could click buttons with their hands or interact with it using their minds.

Izaque made an orange sword that shone and placed it at the right of his waist.

The others, like magical wands of teleportation, glue grappling hooks, personal small jet packs, flying brooms, web-shooters, hoverboards.

They could change their forms, sounds, animations, particles, and effects.

Beings could change the fart gas effect, color, the explosion sound and animation, and other things.
As the New World beings know about every human sense or perception, they can give custom perception proprieties to the objects they create.

Beings can modify those parameters or custom proprieties that exist in the object, or maybe remove some of them.
Like removing the visual propriety of the object, making it only tangible, audible, scentable, and tastable.
Izaque created a new system called "System Maker" before the Hologram Maker, to give the power to create systems to New Worlders.
They also could create holograms that had systems embedded in them, like a teleport hologram and teleport system.

The boy created the Teleport Hologram, which embedded the Teleport System, people could use that system with their hands or mind, without having to open a hologram window.
Beings could type the coordinates of a place in latitude and longitude, the name of an ocean, country, state, and city, and also height.
They could snap their fingers like Thanos while thinking visually or conceptually (names, latitude, and longitude) about a location, and they would be teleported to there.
Beings could modify the action tool to teleport, like hand, mind, how to use the hand to teleport, or what to think about, respectively.
They also could modify the proprieties of the Teleport System in the Animations, Effects, Sounds, and Feelings options of the "Teleport System" option on the "Systems" tab, on the settings window.

Now, with the Visual Interface, beings could go into the settings window or hologram and configure the Signal Receptor.
They could deactivate noisy sounds like cars, bikes, airplanes, construction vehicles, so they do not hear those.
Beings could deactivate visual things like a lantern in their eyes, deactivate their eyes to go blind.

Their bodies could feel and see the magnetic field of Earth, to navigate better, just like some birds.
Izaque and his three friends were receiving a lot of questions through the Question System, they were living on 01/09/2016.

Then, the boys thought about how to implement that into the system, a way for them to help spread and install New World in other universes.
After that, Izaque and his friends created the "New World Ambassador Program", New Worlders could sign up for that program in the "New World Systems" tab on the VI.
They would become "New World Ambassadors", would receive that title, and copies of the new "New World Installation Device".
It was a better version of the Genesis Device, which was physical with holographic proprieties, the NWID was entirely holographic.
Izaque also developed the Inventory System, a system for beings to store physical or holographic things, it had infinite space.
It could be managed using its hologram, it used the Sub-Universe System to create the inventories, one personal inventory, or Sub-Universe for every being.
The ambassadors would receive the NWID into their inventories and would be able to teleport to other universes to install New World there using the "Multiverse Teleportation System" or MTS.
A group of New Worlders was teleported to the New World Enterprise, they walked to the office which has expanded automatically because of the visit.
